Lanna-3L Framework · Internal Codename System

Lanna Codenames
A Living Tribute in Code

ชื่อรหัสล้านนา · Lanna-3L

Every internal module, package, and CLI command carries an authentic Northern Thai name — drawn from traditional craft, ceremony, and community life. The software is built on Lanna soil.

Public API names (LannaBase · LannaAdapt-s · LannaEval) remain unchanged · Internal use only
§ 1 Layer Packages & CLI Commands ชั้นและคำสั่ง

Each layer takes the name of a Lanna place, space, or craft tradition — the metaphor running through every import and command the developer types.

Layer A · lanna_khuang/
ข่วง
Khuang
"Royal Courtyard"
The ข่วงหลวง is the open central courtyard before a temple or palace — the foundational gathering space where community begins all important activity. Raw materials are laid out here, organised, and prepared.

Layer A is where the raw master sheet is laid out, organised, and built into JSONL. Everything downstream begins here.
$ lanna-khuang
Layer B · lanna_kruafai/
ครัวไฟ
Krua Fai
"Traditional Kitchen · The Fire Kitchen"
The ครัวไฟ is the heart of the Lanna home — not just a cooking room but a spiritual and architectural centre. Transformation happens here through fire and skill. Raw ingredients become meals; heat shapes everything.

Layer B is where raw models are transformed: zero-shot, few-shot, LoRA fine-tuning. The GPU is the fire.
$ lanna-kruafai
Layer C · lanna_jorfa/
จ่อฟ้า
Jor Fa
"Sky Finial · Temple Roof Spire"
จ่อฟ้า is the elegant finial atop a Lanna viharn, often carved as a naga. It reaches skyward, connecting earth to the highest realms — a measuring rod of sacred aspiration.

Layer C reaches upward to measure model outputs against ideal benchmarks. Its diagnostic stack connects the earthly generation to the heaven of perfect accuracy.
$ lanna-jorfa
Meta Package · ab_meta/
แอ๊บ
Ab
"Small Lidded Container"
แอ๊บ is a small to medium lidded container used in everyday Lanna life for storing snacks and personal items. It has a removable lid — accessible, portable, essential.

ab_meta holds all shared utilities: schemas, manifest, seed manager, I/O, orchestration. The common tools everyone reaches for.
Pipeline Runner · lanna-fon
ฟ้อน
Fon
"Traditional Lanna Folk Dance"
ฟ้อน is the overarching term for traditional Lanna dance — slow, graceful, and serene, reflecting the gentle character of Northern people. Each movement flows seamlessly into the next.

The pipeline runner orchestrates all layers in sequence. Each step flows into the next with grace. lanna-fon run --pipeline paper1_full.yaml
§ 2 Layer A Modules ข่วง · Data Foundation

Forge tools from Lanna material culture — the act of building, weaving, splitting, coating, and marking.

lanna_khuang/ · formerly lannaforge
สลุง
Salung · Water Bowl
formerly
Anvil
salung_builder.py
A traditional Lanna water bowl, often silver or lacquerware, used in rituals for welcoming guests. As the salung holds and presents pure water, this module holds raw sheet data and presents a clean, structured JSONL corpus to all downstream layers.
→ builds JSONL from master sheet
หูก
Huuk · Loom
formerly
Loom
huuk_validator.py
The traditional Lanna floor loom for weaving textiles. Just as the loom weaves threads into consistent cloth, หูก weaves consistency checks through the JSONL — every row must follow the correct thread.
→ validates JSONL schema
ตอก
Tok · Bamboo Strip
formerly
Cleaver
tok_splitter.py
Bamboo strips used in Lanna basketry; also the tool that splits bamboo cleanly into even strips. ตอก partitions the dataset into train/dev/test — cutting the data into clean, even pieces, like splitting bamboo for weaving.
→ stratified train/dev/test partitioner
รัก
Rak · Lacquer
formerly
Quill
rak_annotator.py
Lacquer from the rak tree — applied in layers to coat and preserve Lanna lacquerware. รัก applies the annotation codebook to each row, coating raw data with a durable layer of pragmatic meaning, preserving intent, tone, and construction type.
→ annotation codebook applicator
ตุง
Tung · Ritual Flag
formerly
Compass
tung_definer.py
A Lanna Buddhist ritual banner, planted to mark direction and aspiration. ตุง plants flags that mark the semantic directions of the benchmark — the 7-field LannaFacets schema (intent, tone, construction type, code-switch, data type, context dependence, location).
→ LannaFacets field definitions (single source of truth)
§ 3 Layer B Modules ครัวไฟ · Adaptation

Smithy tools — the registry, the stencil, the hammer, and the garment that wraps the model.

lanna_kruafai/ · formerly lannasmith
ขันโตก
Khan Tok · Serving Bowl
formerly
Roster
khantok_registry.py
A traditional Lanna low-footed serving bowl used to hold multiple dishes on a single tray — gathering, offering, and communal sharing. ขันโตก holds the 9-model registry: each model is a dish, presented together for the adaptation matrix.
→ 9-model registry (dict[str, ModelSpec])
ฉลุ
Chalu · Carved Stencil
formerly
Stencil
chalu_templater.py
A cut-out stencil pattern used in Lanna wood and metal decoration. ฉลุ constructs prompts from templates — it cuts the fixed pattern, leaving placeholders to be filled with context. The only place prompts are built.
→ prompt templating (system + few-shot assembly)
มุย
Mui · Hammer
formerly
Forgehammer
mui_inference/
A traditional hammer used in Lanna silver and metalcraft. มุย runs inference — it strikes the model to produce outputs, shaping the raw input into a finished generation. Covers both frontier APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek) and open-weights via vLLM.
→ inference runner · api.py + vllm.py
ซิ่น
Sin · Traditional Skirt
formerly
Bellows
sin_finetuner.py
The ซิ่น is the traditional Lanna tubular skirt, woven with intricate patterns — a symbol of Lanna identity. ซิ่น fine-tunes the LoRA adapter: it weaves new patterns (adaptation) into the base fabric (Typhoon2) without re-weaving the entire garment. Paper 1: r=8, α=16.
→ LoRA fine-tuner · PEFT + TRL · Typhoon2 only (Paper 1)
§ 4 Layer C Modules จ่อฟ้า · Evaluation

Scientific instruments — measuring, affirming, aggregating, and catching what falls through.

lanna_jorfa/ · formerly lannascope

① Automatic Diagnostics

สาธุ
Sathu · Amen / Acclamation
formerly
Trident
sathu_robustness.py
A Pali-derived word used as "amen" in Lanna Buddhist chanting — an expression of agreement and affirmation, spoken three times. สาธุ runs the 3P Prompt Robustness protocol, affirming three prompt variations as valid tests. Three affirmations, three prompts.
→ 3P prompt robustness harness
ไตรภูมิ
Trai Phum · Three Realms
formerly
Triplet
traiphum_multiref.py
The traditional Lanna/Thai Buddhist cosmology of three realms (heaven, earth, underworld). ไตรภูมิ scores each hypothesis against three references — measuring the distance across three realms of possible correct translations (3 hyp × 3 refs = 9 ChrF values).
→ Lanna MultiRef scorer via sacrebleu.sentence_chrf
แก้ว
Kaew · Crystal
formerly
Prism
kaew_aggregator.py
Crystal or gemstone — clear and refractive. แก้ว aggregates 9 ChrF values into the three-number Triple-ChrF: chrf_max (optimistic ceiling), chrf_avg (expected performance), chrf_diff (prompt sensitivity). Like a prism, it refracts raw scores into a meaningful spectrum.
→ Triple-ChrF aggregator · {chrf_max, chrf_avg, chrf_diff}
ป่อง
Pong · Gap / Interval
formerly
Caliper
pong_gap.py
In Lanna, ป่อง refers to a gap, cleft, or interval — the space between woven bamboo slices. ป่อง computes the Dialect Gap Score G = chrf_avg(STD) − chrf_avg(NTD), measuring the interval between standard and dialect performance per model.
→ Dialect Gap Score G (headline metric)
แตะ
Tae · Woven Bamboo Panel
formerly
Lattice
tae_slicer.py
A woven bamboo panel made from split strips — used as fencing, walls, or a drying rack (e.g., for tobacco). The gaps allow airflow. แตะ spreads scores across its grid of LannaFacets metadata — each gap between strips is a distinct facet cell (e.g., tone=polite_female), preventing mixing between categories.
→ LannaFacets slice analyzer (7 metadata axes)

② Human Diagnostics

ใบลาน
Bai Lan · Palm-Leaf Manuscript
formerly
Slate
bailan_form.py
The traditional medium for recording Lanna scripture, legal texts, and chronicles — a blank surface awaiting inscription. ใบลาน generates human evaluation forms for annotators: a blank slate for recording judgments on generation quality, fluency, and the four extended Likert axes.
→ human eval form generator (HTML or Label Studio)
ฮอมปอย
Hom Poi · Communal Contribution
formerly
Concord
hompoi_agreement.py
ฮอม = to gather/donate together · ปอย = a merit-making festival. Hom Poi is the Lanna tradition of villagers pooling resources for a communal goal — money, rice, or labour. "Who helps us, we go help them." ฮอมปอย measures how well annotators pool their judgments into a shared reliability score (Krippendorff's α).
→ Krippendorff's α · per-axis (Generation, Fluency, 4 Likert)

③ Error Analysis

แห
Hae · Cast Net
formerly
Sieve
hae_error.py
A traditional Lanna cast net — thrown wide to cover a large area, then pulled in to separate catch from water. แห casts a wide net over all model outputs and pulls up only the failures: worst-k rows by chrf_avg, prompt-sensitivity outliers (high chrf_diff), tone-flip cases, construction-loss, and CD-drop.
→ worst-k · tone-flip · construction-loss · CD-drop
§ 5 Meta Modules แอ๊บ · Shared Utilities

The people and laws that hold the community together — bridge-keepers, headmen, stones, and moral codes.

ab_meta/ · formerly lannakit
ปู่จ๋าน
Pu Jan · Lay Minister
formerly
io.py
pujan_io.py
ปู่จ๋าน is the Lanna lay minister — a former monk who bridges the temple and the villagers, carrying messages, coordinating rituals, and facilitating exchange between realms. ปู่จ๋าน handles all JSONL read/write and hashing: the bridge that carries data in and out of the system.
→ JSONL read/write · SHA-256 hashing helpers
ป้อหลวง
Po Luang · Village Headman
formerly
orchestrate.py
poluang_orchestrate.py
ป้อหลวง is the Northern Thai village headman — a fatherly figure who settles disputes, leads community activities, and serves as the official liaison between government and people. ป้อหลวง leads the entire pipeline, reading YAML configs and coordinating all layers in sequence.
→ lanna-fon run entrypoint · pipeline orchestration
มะหิน
Mahin · Stone
formerly
loomstone.py
mahin_seed.py
มะหิน is the Lanna word for stone — solid, unchanging, the same every time. มะหิน is the deterministic seed manager: seed_all(42) seeds numpy, torch, random, and transformers in one call. The stone anchor that makes experiments reproducible.
→ Loomstone.seed_all() · default seed = 42
ปี้
Pee · Tag / Mark
formerly
manifest.py
pee_manifest.py
ปี้ is a mark or tag left to identify ownership or provenance — a small stamp of identity. Every experiment produces a ปี้: a manifest stamped with config, data hashes, git SHA, tooling versions, and hardware. No output is accepted without its tag.
→ run manifest · config + SHA + timestamps
ฮีต
Heet · Customary Law
formerly
schema.py
heet_schema.py
ฮีต (from Pali charita) are the customary moral codes and traditions passed down through Lanna generations — the "right way" of living. ฮีต defines the Pydantic schemas that enforce the right structure for every data row: DatasetRow, OutputRow, Annotation, Provenance. No row is accepted unless it follows the ฮีต.
→ Pydantic schemas · canonical data types

§ 8 Where Paper Readers See This ตำแหน่งในงานวิจัย

The public API names (LannaBase, LannaAdapt-s, LannaEval) remain unchanged in the paper. The Lanna codenames appear in three places, at increasing depth.

Core principle: Paper readers get a taste in the main text. Those who want depth go to supplementary materials or the repository. No page limit is exceeded.
Main paper
(compact table)
A small table in the Implementation section or Appendix A listing the 3 layer packages + 6–8 key modules. One line each: Thai · Romanisation · English meaning · Software role. Gives reviewers immediate cultural flavour without bulk.
Supplementary
materials (PDF)
Full glossary with 2–3 sentence cultural descriptions for all 27 codenames. Formatted as a 2-page reference document. Readers who are curious about cultural grounding can access it without cluttering the main paper.
Repository
GLOSSARY.md
This document — full descriptions, Thai script, romanisation, cultural role, software function, and file name. Cross-referenced from the paper's Data & Code Availability statement: "A glossary of Lanna codenames is provided in GLOSSARY.md."
